Ingredients

  • 2 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 1/8 tsp allspice
  • 1 or 2 Knorr beef bouillon cubes
  • Flour & water slurry

Steps

  1. Mix everything EXCEPT bouillon cubes. Form into 2 inch meatballs.
  2. Brown in large frying pan, on all sides, until all pink on the inside is gone. (Works much faster with 2 pans.)
  3. Add enough water to make enough gravy to serve with your meatballs. (Usually water is about halfway up the meatballs.)
  4. Bring to a boil, scrape bottom of the pan to free up the cracklings. Add one or two bouillon cubes (depending on how much gravy you are making.)
  5. Cook for a few minutes and then thicken gravy with a slurry (flour and water mixture).
