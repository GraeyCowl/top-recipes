Ingrediants

  • 16 ounces refrigerated Cheese Tortellini
  • 12 ounce good quality Marinara Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Ranch Dressing
  • 1/2 Cup Celery
  • 1/2 Cup Cucumber
  • 1/2 Cup Niblet Corn
  • 1 Tbsp dried Onion Flakes
  • 2 tsp dried Basil
  • Cover and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Steps

  1. Cook tortellini according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine marinara sauce and ranch dressing; whisk to blend.
  3. Add in tortellini, celery, cucumber, niblet corn, onion flakes, and dried basil; toss to coat.
  4. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
