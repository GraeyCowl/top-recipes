Sweet Lemon Curry Chicken

Description

Adaptation of Norene Gilletz's Second Helping Please: Lemon Barbecue Chicken. Really tasty with wings or thighs.

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs chicken pieces
  • Shake'n Bake Coating Mix
  • 4 Tbsp lemon (or lime) juice (fresh is best but bottled works)
  • 1/2 Cup Celery
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Coat chicken as directed on package.
  3. Place skin side down on a parchment paper covered cookie sheet. Bake at 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
  4. Turn chicken over.
  5. Combine remaining ingredients, pour over chicken.
  6. Bake another 30 minutes or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
  7. Baste several times.
